Registration and deregistration

From 1 July 2018 Danish legislation requires tattooing establishments in Denmark to register at the Danish Safety Technology Authority.


If you are the owner of one or more tattooing establishments in Denmark, you must register each establishment to the Danish National Registry of Tattooing Establishments. When registered, you are required to keep your information on the establishment updated at all times. Should you wish to close the establishment, you will need to deregister the establishment from the Danish national registry of tattooing establishments.

You also have to register all the tattooists working in the establishment, including yourself if you work as a tattooist. Do also remember to change the registration if new tattooists are hired or if tattooists quit.

New tattooing establishments
Tattooing establishments that are established 1 July 2018 or later have to register at the Danish Safety Technology Authority before starting the tattooing activities. 

You can use the registration form here to register in the supervisory scheme.
You will be charged an annual fee - DKK 5,776 - for registration and supervision from the Danish Safety Technology Authority.

Existing tattooing establishments
Tattooing establishments established prior to 1 July 2018 can choose between two registration schemes until 31 December 2019.

Advisory Scheme:
This is a provisional scheme, and is to be regarded as a transition to the current Supervisory Scheme. The Danish Safety Technology Authority visits tattooing establishments to advise on the new statutory requirements that must be complied with.

The fee for registering in the advisory scheme for the first year (2019) is DKK 1,200.

Supervisory Scheme:
The full and current Supervisory Scheme comprises inspection visits by the Danish Safety Technology Authority.

The annual fee is DKK 5,776 for registration in the supervisory scheme. It covers the costs for registration and supervision from the Danish Safety Technology Authority.

Changes and deregistration
If you have changes concerning your tattooing establishment or if you want to deregister you must fill in a specific form.
Link to Business in Denmark

Temporary tattooing establishments

If a place is only used for tattooing in a limited time you must register as a temporary tattooing establishment. You also have to register the tattooists working in the temporary tattooing establishment. You can use the same form as the permanent tattooing establishments for registration. Remember to de-register after the event. 

You must register all the tattoists with name and contact information. If there are many of them, you can use a separate form and upload it with your registration form.
