Authorisation and approval

The legislation covering the electricity, plumbing, gas and sewage installation fields regulates which types of installation work require authorisation or approval – and which can be carried out without authorisation or approval.


Authorisation or approval ensures that electricity, water and drainage, gas and sewage installations are carried out and serviced correctly for the sake of health, safety and the environment.

General information

The Danish Safety Technology Authority controls the authorisations within the electricity, plumbing and sewage installation areas as well as the company approvals within the gas area. The authorisation is given to the company, but a prerequisite is the employment of one or more individuals having professional responsibility.
