Tobacco

Here you can see the information about the legislation in the tobacco field. You will find information about the requirements for reporting and notification.


Rules and regulations on tobacco

The EU has adopted a new tobacco directive – this means a legislation in the tobacco field.

Reporting and notification

Read more about the requirements for reporting and notification.

Marketing ban of particular types of tobacco

It is banned to market tobacco, which is consumed orally. This ban includes e.g. snuff. As of 20 May 2020, it will be banned to market cigarettes and twist tobacco with a characteristic aroma such as menthol.

Labelling and health warnings

Requirements for labelling and health warnings.

Cross frontier distance selling

If you want to market tobacco to consumers by cross frontier distance selling, you shall be registered with the Danish Safety Technology Authority.

The Danish Safety Technology Authority’s control

The Danish Safety Technology Authority control the tobacco field.

Fees

The Danish Safety Technology Authority’s costs for case handling and market surveillance in the tobacco field will be covered by fees. The fees shall cover the expected costs for e.g. the Danish Safety Technology Authority’s administration of the reporting- and notification system and the control, which the authority shall carry out.

Transitional rules

Transitional rules shall ensure a smooth phase-in of the new rules.
