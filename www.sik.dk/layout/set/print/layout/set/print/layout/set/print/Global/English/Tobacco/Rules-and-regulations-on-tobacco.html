Rules and regulations on tobacco

The EU has adopted a new tobacco directive – this means a legislation in the tobacco field.


New legislation in Denmark
The new EU directive means a new legislation in Denmark. Act on tobacco, etc. shall apply to tobacco and related products.

The directive
The tobacco directive regulates tobacco and related products. The purpose of the directive is to ensure a more homogenous legislation between the Member States in the tobacco field. The new thing is that it will be banned to market cigarettes and twist tobacco with a characteristic aroma.

Moreover, an obligation to notify new categories of tobacco and a liability to register the tobacco are implemented for the retailers who market tobacco to the consumers in this country or to another EU/EEA country by means of cross frontier distance selling. The directive implements also rules for marketing of related products, including herbal smoking products.

Date of commencement
The new rules for tobacco commences on 9 June 2016.
