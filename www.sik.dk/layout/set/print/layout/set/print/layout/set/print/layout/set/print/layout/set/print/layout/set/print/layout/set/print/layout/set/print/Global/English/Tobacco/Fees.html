Fees

The Danish Safety Technology Authority’s costs for case handling and market surveillance in the tobacco field will be covered by fees. The fees shall cover the expected costs for e.g. the Danish Safety Technology Authority’s administration of the reporting- and notification system and the control, which the authority shall carry out.


Tobacco fees
The fee is charged from manufacturers and importers of tobacco covered by the reporting duty of the law. The fee is divided on the basis of the individual market share on the basis of the tobacco fee, which has been reported to SKAT.

It is estimated that the tobacco fee comprises 9.0m DKK in average over the first four years.

What shall the fees cover?
The fees shall cover the Danish Safety Technology Authority’s costs for e.g. case handling, market control, including costs for external laboratory tests, market analysis and system support.

Charge of the fees
The fees are charged twice a year. An amount is charged on account as of 30 June each year and final settlement and charge of fees are made on 31 December each year.

Fees for new categories of tobacco and herbal smoking products
Notification to the Danish Safety Technology Authority of a new category of tobacco or reporting of an herbal smoking product costs DKK 36,900 each product. Subsequently, an annual fee of DKK 14,700 shall be paid to maintain the notification or the reporting of a product.

Products, for which notification fee, reporting fee or maintenance fee already have been paid by another manufacturer or importer, are excepted from the fee duty. The exemption shall apply only during the periods, where the other manufacturer or importer pays the fee.

In the cases where a new category of tobacco after a period in the market is transferred to be covered by the rules of tobacco fee, the tobacco in question is exempted from the maintenance fee.

What the fees shall cover
The fees shall cover the costs for e.g. case handling of the notification of new categories of tobacco and reporting of herbal smoking products and market control for the following period of these products.

The maintenance fee shall cover the Danish Safety Technology Authority’s running costs for administration and market control of existing products which are marketed in the market for long periods.

Payment of the fees
Fees for notification of a new category of tobacco or reporting of an herbal smoking product are paid to the Danish Safety Technology Authority on www.sik.dk in relation to the notification or the reporting.

The maintenance fee for the notification or the reporting is paid once a year and is due for payment each year on the date, where confirmation of correct notification has been issued or where reporting has taken place.
