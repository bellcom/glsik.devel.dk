Application forms

If you have completed a qualification outside Denmark (in another EU or EEA country) and wish to obtain authorisation or an approval within the gas field in Denmark, you will find the relevant application forms here.


Please note:

You must always submit at least two applications: one for the company and one for the technically responsible person.

All documentation has to be sent in PDF format.

Applications for the individual

Notification of temporary and occasional pursuit of profession for individuals:

  • Electrical installations field (PDF)
  • Plumbing and sanitation field (PDF)
  • Sewage installations field (PDF)
  • Gas field (PDF)
  • Cable installer and pipe fitter (PDF)
  • Operator of electrical plants (PDF)

Application for working permanently in Denmark as an individual:

  • Electrical installations field (PDF)

Fee

  • Authorisation for the company
  • Working permanently as an individual

The fee is always charged for the company application. If a company wants to set up permanently in Denmark, the fee is also charged for the technically responsible person .
