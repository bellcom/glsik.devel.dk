Reporting and notification

Read more about the requirements for reporting and notification.


Report information about tobacco with the Danish Safety Technology Authority and the European Commission
Manufacturers and importers of tobacco, which is marketed in Denmark, must report information about all ingredients in the tobacco to the Danish Safety Technology Authority.

Information about ingredients and emissions shall be reported for all tobacco
Regarding information about which information to be reported, reference is made to section 3 of ”Registrering af oplysninger om tobaksvarer til Sikkerhedsstyrelsen og Europa-Kommissionen” in Act on tobacco, etc.

Report on examination of additives
Manufacturers and importers shall carry out comprehensive examinations of additives entered on the prioritised list of the Commission. A report shall be drawn up and forwarded to the Danish Safety Technology Authority and the Commission.

Requirements for approval and control of laboratories which measure the emission content
Upon application, the Danish Safety Technology Authority can approve laboratories to control the measuring of the emission content of tar, nicotine and carbon monoxide in cigarettes. In order to obtain an approval, the laboratory shall be accredited by DANAK, The Danish Accreditation Fund, or by an equivalent other European accreditation body. A laboratory, which wants an approval or has been approved, may not be owned or directly or indirectly controlled by the tobacco industry.

Annual reporting
Manufacturers and importers of tobacco, which is marketed in this country, shall for production, which they market, no later than on 1 May each year, report information about sales volume for the precedent year to the Danish Safety Technology Authority. You should be aware that data for the current year must be submitted as a supplement to any previously submitted data. Please ensure that data submitted for previous years remain visible

Herbal smoking products shall be reported to the Danish Safety Technology Authority
Manufacturers and importers, who market herbal smoking products, shall report information about the composition of the products to the Danish Safety Technology Authority.

New categories of tobacco shall be notified to the Danish Safety Technology Authority
New categories of tobacco may be marketed in this country only if they have been notified to the Danish Safety Technology Authority. Notification shall be given of each manufacturer and importer who wants to market a new category of tobacco. This shall take place no later than 6 (six) months before it shall be marketed.

All notifications and reporting regarding tobacco to the Danish Safety Technology Authority shall be made digitally through the joint EU-system, EU-CEG - Central Entry Gate.
