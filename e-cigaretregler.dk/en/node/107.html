Report significant changes to e-cigarette products 
When an already registered product have significant changes, please report a new product. The legal basis is contained in Section 3 of the Order on notification of electronic cigarettes and refill containers, etc.
In EU-CEG, you must choose Submission type no. 2 ’Modification of information on a previously reported product’. The product is thereby assigned a new product ID (EC ID) with a link to the previous product.

Determination of significant changes
We determine whether a submission includes significant changes. Concerning e-cigarettes, the assessment is based on the following specifications from the submission in EU-CEG:
• Liquid Volume Capacity
• Airflow adjustable
• Nicotine Concentration     
• Wick changeable
• Battery Type  
• Microprocessor
• Voltage Wattage adjustable  
• Coil Composition
• Wattage  
• Coil Resistance
• Wattage upper range

 

Concerning refill containers, the assessment is based on the following specifications from the submission in EU-CEG:
• Liquid Volume Capacity    
• Ingredients
• Nicotine Concentration  
• Recipe Quantity

In principle it is a significant change when the above specifications are changed in an update of a product that is already on the List of registered products.
We are focused on significant changes of previously submitted products and we will select submissions for a special review based on a risk-based approach. This review includes an evaluation of whether the product (still) should be excepted from paying a fee.
