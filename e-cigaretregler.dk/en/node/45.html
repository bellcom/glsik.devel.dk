Age limit

You must be 18 years to legally buy electronic cigarettes and refillable containers with or without nicotine in Denmark.

The retailers are responsible that electronic cigarettes and refillable containers with or without nicotine are not being sold to persons under 18 years.

You must be prepared to present valid photo ID card (e.g. passport or driving license) when you buy electronic cigarettes.

 

 

The same applies when you buy electronic cigarettes and refillable containers on the internet. Here the retailers are also obliged to have an age control system.

For more information contact the Ministry of Health.
